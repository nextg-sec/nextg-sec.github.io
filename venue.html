Charles B. Wang Center, Stony Brook University

The NextG-Sec workshop will be held at the Charles B. Wang Center on the campus of Stony Brook University in Stony Brook, New York.

Venue Information

Opened in 2002, the Wang Center is a cultural hub dedicated to promoting an understanding of Asian and Asian American cultures. This impressive 120,000-square-foot facility was designed by architect P.H. Tuan and features a distinctive 100-foot octagonal pagoda tower. Inside, the center houses conference halls, auditoriums, and galleries, all surrounding a beautiful outdoor garden.

Location

The Charles B. Wang Center is conveniently located on the Stony Brook University campus. You can find the exact location by searching "Charles B. Wang Center Stony Brook" in Google Maps or Apple Maps.

Stony Brook University is located on Long Island, about 50 miles east of New York City. The campus is accessible by car and public transportation. For more information about traveling to Stony Brook, visit the Stony Brook University website.
